CLUBS IN THE SCHOOL


Different Clubs And Their Details:


Eco Club: Fostering a sense of responsibility for our planet in students and staff.

Road Safety Club: Fostering a sense of vigilance, responsibility, and awareness for safety.


Anti-Drug Abuse Club: Fostering a sense of resilience and empowerment to create a community dedicated to a drug-free lifestyle


Disaster Management Club: Fostering a sense of resilience and empowerment to create a community prepared for and capable of effective disaster management.


Anti-Sexual Harassment Committee: Fostering a sense of awareness, empathy, and accountability to create a safe and inclusive environment.


Red Cross Club: Fostering a culture of humanitarianism and community service to cultivate a compassionate and responsive environment.

Electoral Literacy Club: Fostering a sense of civic responsibility and awareness to create a informed and active participation in the democratic process.


Sports Club: Fostering a sense of teamwork, discipline, and sportsmanship to create a dynamic and inclusive Sports Club for enthusiasts of all levels.


Mid-Day Meal: Fostering a sense of nutrition and community support to create a Mid-Day Meal initiative to ensure every child has access to a nourishing meal.




Some Organisations And Schemes:


National Service Scheme: Fostering a sense of social responsibility and community service within our NSS, working towards positive change.


National Cadet Corps: Fostering a sense of discipline and patriotism to create responsible citizens within our NCC unit.


Scout And Guide: Fostering a sense of preparedness and character development within our Scout and Guide community.
