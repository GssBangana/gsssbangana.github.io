Eco Club

Introduction: At GSSS Bangana, we believe in the power of collective action to create a sustainable and environmentally conscious community. The Eco Club is at the forefront of this mission, dedicated to fostering a sense of responsibility for our planet among students and staff.

Our Mission: The Eco Club is committed to promoting environmental awareness, sustainable practices, and conservation efforts within the school and the broader community. Through a variety of engaging initiatives, we aim to inspire positive change and contribute to a greener, more eco-friendly future.
